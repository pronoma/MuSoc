Introduction

The Music Society is a performing club in BITS Goa, comprising music lovers, talented vocalists and musicians who are inclined in making their own covers, as well as creating their own music

The club hosts 2-3 music events in the college per semester, have participated and won in several inter-college competitions, and also have several informal jam sessions for the students.

The Music Society, along with other 2 other clubs (Synchronoise and Srutilaya) are responsible for maintaining a strong music culture within campus
